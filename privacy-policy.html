Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2021

At Friends of Whittier Narrows, your privacy and trust are important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our archival website.

1. What We Collect

As an archival and informational website, we collect minimal personal data. We may collect:

  • Name, email address, and message content (only if you contact us via our accessibility form or other direct communication)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to:

  • Respond to your inquiries or feedback
  • Improve the accessibility and experience of our website

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Use of Third-Party Tools

We utilize tools like:

  • Formspree (for securely processing contact and accessibility feedback forms)

These platforms have their own privacy policies which you should review when using them.

4. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to any personal data you have submitted to us
  • Ask us to update or delete your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the form on our Accessibility page.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information but cannot guarantee complete security. Please avoid sharing highly sensitive data via unencrypted forms.
